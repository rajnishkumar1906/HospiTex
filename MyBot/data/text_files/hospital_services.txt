=== HOSPITAL SERVICES OVERVIEW ===

HospiTex Hospital Management System:
HospiTex is a comprehensive hospital management system with three main dashboards: Patient, Doctor, and Diagnostic. The system facilitates seamless communication and coordination between all parties.

Patient Services:
Patients can book appointments, view medical records, access diagnostic reports, contact doctors, request ambulance services, and manage their health information through the patient dashboard.

Doctor Services:
Doctors manage appointments, access patient records, create prescriptions, document consultations, recommend diagnostic tests, and communicate with patients through the doctor dashboard.

Diagnostic Services:
Diagnostic centers manage test bookings, process samples, generate reports, share results with doctors and patients, and maintain quality standards through the diagnostic dashboard.

User Authentication:
All users must register and verify their accounts. The system supports different user roles: Patient, Doctor, Diagnostic, and Admin. Email verification is required for account activation.

Profile Management:
Users can update their profiles including personal information, contact details, and preferences. Doctors can add their specialties, qualifications, and availability information.

Notification System:
The system sends automatic notifications for appointment confirmations, test results, prescription updates, and important announcements. Notifications are sent via email and displayed in dashboards.

Medical Records:
All medical information is securely stored including consultation notes, prescriptions, diagnostic reports, and appointment history. Patients and authorized doctors can access these records.

Data Security:
Patient data is protected with secure authentication and encryption. Only authorized personnel can access medical records, ensuring patient privacy and confidentiality.

Multi-user Support:
The system supports multiple patients, doctors, and diagnostic centers simultaneously. Each user has their own dashboard with role-specific features and permissions.

Integration:
All three dashboards are integrated, allowing seamless data flow between patients, doctors, and diagnostic centers. Information is synchronized in real-time.

Mobile Accessibility:
The system is accessible through web browsers on desktop and mobile devices, ensuring users can access services from anywhere.

Support Services:
The system includes support for ambulance services, emergency contacts, and help documentation. Patients can request assistance through various channels.
