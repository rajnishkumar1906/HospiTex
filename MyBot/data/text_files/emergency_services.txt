=== EMERGENCY SERVICES ===

Emergency Helpline:
For medical emergencies, patients can call the emergency helpline available 24/7. The helpline connects patients with emergency medical services and provides immediate assistance.

Ambulance Service:
Patients can request ambulance services through the patient dashboard. The ambulance service is available for emergencies and can be booked for non-emergency patient transport. Real-time tracking may be available.

Emergency Appointments:
In case of medical emergencies, patients can request urgent appointments. Doctors prioritize emergency cases and provide immediate consultation when available.

Emergency Diagnostic Tests:
For critical conditions, diagnostic centers prioritize emergency tests. Results are expedited and shared immediately with doctors for urgent medical decisions.

Emergency Contact:
Patients can add emergency contacts in their profile. In case of emergencies, these contacts can be notified and authorized to access medical information if needed.

Triage System:
The system supports triage for emergency cases, helping prioritize patients based on severity. Critical cases receive immediate attention.

Emergency Protocols:
HospiTex follows standard emergency medical protocols. All emergency services are coordinated to ensure quick response and appropriate care.

After-hours Services:
Emergency services are available 24/7. Doctors on call can be contacted for urgent consultations outside regular hours.

Emergency Medical Records:
Emergency medical personnel can access critical patient information including allergies, current medications, and medical history to provide appropriate emergency care.

Patient Status:
In emergencies, patient status can be updated to critical, and all relevant medical staff are notified immediately.

Coordination:
Emergency services coordinate with doctors, diagnostic centers, and ambulance services to ensure comprehensive emergency care.
