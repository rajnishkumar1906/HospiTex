=== DIAGNOSTIC SERVICES AND TESTS ===

Diagnostic Center Overview:
Diagnostic centers provide various medical tests including blood tests, imaging, pathology, radiology, and specialized diagnostic procedures. Patients can contact diagnostic centers through the diagnostic dashboard.

Test Booking:
Patients can book diagnostic tests through the diagnostic section. Select the type of test needed, choose a date and time, and receive confirmation. Some tests may require doctor recommendations.

Test Preparation:
Different tests have specific preparation requirements. Fasting may be required for blood tests, while imaging tests may have dietary restrictions. Diagnostic centers provide detailed preparation instructions when booking.

Test Results:
Test results are available through the diagnostic dashboard. Results are typically available within 24-48 hours depending on the test type. Patients receive notifications when results are ready.

Report Sharing:
Diagnostic reports can be shared directly with doctors through the system. Doctors receive automatic notifications when new reports are available for their patients.

Common Diagnostic Tests:
Blood tests include complete blood count (CBC), lipid profile, blood sugar, liver function, kidney function, and thyroid tests. Imaging includes X-rays, CT scans, MRI, and ultrasound. Pathology includes biopsy and cytology tests.

Specialized Tests:
Specialized diagnostic tests include cardiac stress tests, endoscopy, colonoscopy, mammography, and genetic testing. These require specific scheduling and preparation.

Test Packages:
Diagnostic centers offer health checkup packages that include multiple tests at discounted rates. These packages are useful for comprehensive health assessments.

Home Sample Collection:
For convenience, some diagnostic centers offer home sample collection services. Patients can schedule home visits for sample collection, especially for elderly or mobility-impaired patients.

Report Interpretation:
Diagnostic reports include detailed results with reference ranges. Patients can view reports in the dashboard, and doctors can provide interpretation during consultations.

Follow-up Tests:
Based on initial test results, doctors may recommend follow-up tests. These can be scheduled directly through the diagnostic section with doctor recommendations attached.

Quality Assurance:
All diagnostic centers maintain quality standards and certifications. Test results are accurate and reliable, meeting medical laboratory standards.

Emergency Tests:
For urgent cases, diagnostic centers prioritize emergency tests. Results are expedited and shared immediately with doctors and patients.
